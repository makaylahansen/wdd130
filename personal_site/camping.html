Having the perfect camping spot is the most important. After all, your tent is going to be your temporary home. If you choose the wrong spot, you could find yourself sleeping on rocks or having loud neighbors. However, if you choose the right spot, you could have the best sleep of your life and wake up to the most heavenly view.

If you ever get the chance to hike on private property, say yes! You will not regret your choice. It is very convenient to choose your own camping spot on soft ground. You also don’t have to worry about any nosy neighbors. As amazing as it is, camping on private property is not always an option. Some thinks to check before you book a camping spot are:

  • Make sure it is easily accessible. You never know when a potential dirt road could be a problem.
  • If possible, choose a spot on the outer ring. Even though it is a longer walk to the bathroom, it helps you feel more at one with nature. You can be surrounded by it and wake up to a nice view rather than a bunch of tents in your face.
  • Always always make sure that your camping spot will fit the amount of people on your trip. You don’t want to feel cramped.
